STATATHON 2025 – A Data Journey Towards Viksit Bharat

In a major push to harness the power of data for nation-building, the Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation (MoSPI), in collaboration with the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and the Ministry of Education’s Innovation Cell (MIC), has announced the launch of STATATHON 2025 – A Data Journey for Viksit Bharat. This prestigious national-level innovation challenge invites students, research scholars, and startups to design AI, data science, and technology-driven solutions addressing real-world challenges curated by MoSPI.

The competition will focus on five high-impact problem statements aligned with national priorities, ranging from improving statistical systems to enabling data-informed governance. In an unprecedented show of support, the organisers will provide seed funding of ₹50,000 to each of the 25 shortlisted teams to develop their proof-of-concept solutions. Winners and runners-up in each category stand to gain attractive cash prizes, national recognition, and mentorship from leading experts in the field.

The deadline for registration and idea submission is 14th August 2025, and entries can be submitted through the official portal at gc.mic.gov.in. Institutes, IICs, and incubators have been urged to circulate the announcement widely among students, faculty, and startups, while also providing necessary guidance and bonafide certificates (sample formats are available on the STATATHON portal).

To address participant queries, a doubt-clearing session will be held on Friday, 8th August 2025 at 3:00 PM. Organisers believe this initiative will not only foster innovation but also play a pivotal role in shaping data-driven solutions for a developed India.

Independence Day Celebrations

The University warmly invites all students, faculty, and staff to join the Independence Day Celebrations on Friday, August 15, 2025 at the Main Quadrangle. Proceedings will commence at 8:00 AM with the ceremonial Flag Hoisting, followed by a cultural programme showcasing patriotic performances, music, and dance that reflect the country’s diverse heritage.

In addition, small events with exciting prizes and interactive sessions will be held throughout the morning to mark the occasion. Refreshments will be served to all attendees. All are encouraged to participate and celebrate the enduring spirit of unity and freedom together.

Blood Donation Camp

The NSS and YRC Units of KARE, in collaboration with Government Hospital, Virudhunagar, will host a Blood Donation Camp on Saturday, August 23, 2025, from 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM at KMCH, Krishnankoil. Donors will undergo a quick health check-up before contributing.

All students are encouraged to participate. Appreciation certificates will be presented by the Government of Tamil Nadu to all donors.

Red Venture – A Plastic-Free Mission

The Youth Red Cross (YRC), KARE, is organising Red Venture – A Plastic-Free Mission at Kochi & Kanthaloor on September 5–6, 2025. The initiative focuses on environmental conservation through awareness drives, beach clean-ups, and tree plantation activities.

Student registrations will be open at Dr. K.S. Krishnan Auditorium from August 6 to September 4, 2025, between 4:00 PM and 6:00 PM.

Intercollegiate Cricket Tournament

The annual Intercollegiate Cricket Tournament will commence this September at the University Grounds. Matches will be held over several weekends to allow maximum participation and spectator engagement.

Teams from across the region will compete for the championship title, promising thrilling matches, exceptional sportsmanship, and a spirited campus atmosphere throughout the event. Students are welcome to volunteer in organising committees and cheer for their favourite teams.
